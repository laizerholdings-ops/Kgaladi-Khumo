Kgaladi and Khumo Holdings (Pty) Ltd was founded with a singular purpose: to provide professionalized, reliable, and compliant services to the South African residential and corporate sectors. We identified a gap where clients often had to choose between affordability and quality—we aim to deliver both.

Based in Tembisa and serving the broader Gauteng region, we are more than just a contractor; we are a partner in growth. Our company operates under the guiding principle of "Built with Excellence." This is not just a slogan; it is a technical requirement.

From SABS-approved materials used in our structural builds to the environmentally friendly, high-grade chemicals used by our cleaning division, everything is chosen for its longevity and performance. We bridge the gap between high-level engineering and everyday service delivery.
